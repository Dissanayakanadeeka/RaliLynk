RailLynk

Train Station Notifier and Digital Ticketing System For Sri Lanka Railway

Introduction

Inefficiencies in ticketing and navigation within Sri Lanka's railway system lead to delays, confusion, and revenue loss. This project proposes a digital solution integrating station notifications, ticketing, and multilingual support.
